Lyrics Prompter

Lyrics prompter for live musicians — synced to your backing tracks

Velvet Show includes a professional lyrics prompter built directly into your backing track workflow. Lyrics and chords scroll automatically as the music plays — you never touch a scroll wheel or tap a button mid-song.

How the lyrics prompter works

Each song in Velvet Show has a timeline. On that timeline, you place lyric blocks at the exact position where they should appear. When the backing track plays, the lyrics scroll in sync — automatically. Chords, key changes and stage notes appear at exactly the right moment.

  • Import lyrics from plain text in one click
  • Place lyric blocks directly on the waveform timeline
  • Chords and key changes synced to the music
  • Stage notes and arrangement reminders on the same timeline
  • Large type, high contrast — readable under stage lighting
  • Stage screen output to iPad or second monitor
  • Velvet Remote shows lyrics on iPad — full screen, scrolls automatically

iPad as a stage prompter

Velvet Show outputs to a second display — your iPad, a monitor at the back of the stage, or any external screen. The stage screen shows the current song title, a countdown timer, the next song, and the current lyric block in large readable type. You can also use Velvet Remote on your iPad: it connects to Velvet Show over local WiFi and displays lyrics in real time, with playback controls at the bottom.

No more scrolling PDFs mid-song

If you've been managing lyrics in a PDF viewer, a notes app or a second device you manually scroll during performance — Velvet Show replaces all of that. One app, one timeline, everything in sync.
