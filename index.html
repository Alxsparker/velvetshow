MaestroDMX Integration

MaestroDMX handles the lights.
Velvet Show runs the show.

Velvet Show connects directly to MaestroDMX — your lighting engine stays in control, while Velvet Show fires cues automatically.

  • Automatic cue triggering via MIDI and OSC
  • Import MaestroDMX cue list
  • Manual cue control
  • Master Brightness control
  • MIDI mode, OSC mode, or both simultaneously

MaestroDMX remains the DMX lighting engine. Velvet Show is the show control center that drives it.

Panic Mode

One shortcut. Everything goes quiet.

If a string breaks or the room needs a moment — press PANIC. Audio stops, the stage screen switches to a calm safety display.
