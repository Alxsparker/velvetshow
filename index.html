Stage Notes & Lyrics

Everything you need, exactly when you need it.

Lyrics, chords, reminders and stage directions are timed to the waveform and surface automatically as the song plays. No more sticky notes on the monitor, no more scrolling a PDF mid-song.

  • Drop lyric blocks directly onto the waveform
  • Import lyrics from text in one click
  • Cue points for tempo, key and chord changes
  • BPM display and tap-tempo per song

Stage Screen

A confidence screen for you, or your whole band.

Send lyrics, chords and stage notes to an iPad, a second monitor, or a screen at the back of the stage. Large type, high contrast, zero distractions — readable from across the room under any lighting.

  • Mirror or extend to any external display
  • Adjustable type size for distance and lighting
  • Shows current and next song automatically
  • Independent from your main control window

Panic Mode

One shortcut. Everything goes quiet.

If a string breaks, a cable comes loose, or the room needs a moment — press PANIC. The stage screen switches instantly to a calm safety display, audio stops, and the show pauses cleanly until you're ready to continue.

⏻ PANIC

⌥ + ⌘ + P — bound to one key, reachable in the dark.

Smooth Transitions

From cocktail to dancefloor without a single cut.

Choose how one song moves into the next — fade, slow fade, or a filtered crossfade — and Velvet Show handles the timing. Built for the moments where the room's energy needs to shift without anyone noticing the seam.

  • Fade, slow fade and filter-sweep transitions
  • Auto-advance with a visible "Next · Auto" cue
  • Preview the outgoing and incoming track together
  • Designed for ceremonies, cocktails and lounge sets
