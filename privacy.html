Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 15, 2026

Velvet Show and Velvet Remote do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data. No account is required. No analytics. No tracking.

Velvet Show — macOS

Velvet Show is a macOS application that runs entirely on your local machine. It reads from a local SQLite database (ShowBuddy) and stores all its own data in a local JSON file on your Mac.

  • No data is sent to any server.
  • No analytics or crash reporting is enabled.
  • No account or registration is required.
  • The app requests access to your local audio files folder (macOS sandbox) — this access stays on your device.

Velvet Remote — iPhone & iPad

Velvet Remote is a companion app for Velvet Show. It communicates exclusively with Velvet Show running on a Mac on your local network or via USB cable.

  • No data is sent to any external server.
  • No personal information is collected or stored.
  • No account or registration is required.
  • The app uses the Local Network permission to discover Velvet Show via Bonjour on your local network. This permission is used solely for device discovery and does not transmit any personal data.
  • The last connected server name is stored locally on the device to enable automatic reconnection. This information never leaves your device.
